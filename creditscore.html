CIBIL SCORE AND REPORT


Whenever you apply for a loan, lenders check your CIBIL Score and Report.
Records of your payments pertaining to loans and credit cards are submitted to
CIBIL by banks and other lenders on a monthly basis.
This information forms the basis of your CIBIL Score and
Report which is provided to lenders in order to help them evaluate and approve your loan applications.
Monitoring your CIBIL Score provides you with control over your credit information.
This in turn helps ensure that you maintain your score above 750.
The higher your score, the higher are the chances of your loan application getting approved!
